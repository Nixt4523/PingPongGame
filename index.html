How to Play..!

Use your mouse to move your Paddle, and try to bounce incoming Ball and continue playing the Game.

Don't miss the Ball or you'll lose the Game, Ball speed will increase as game continues.

There will be 3 rounds in Total, If you win 2 rounds you win the Game or if you lose Computer wins the Game.
